Cinema Multiplex

Cinema Multiplex is a collection of films that have been rescrambled to exercise and stretch that multitasking-oriented part of the mind. N Chapters from source films were divided into their respective shots, then shots were then arranged such that Ch 1 Shot 1 → Ch 2 Shot 1 → Ch N Shot 1 → ... → Ch 1 Shot 2 → Ch 2 Shot 2 ... and so on. From A1A2A3B1B2B3C1C2C3 To A1B1C1A2B2C2A3B3C3: for us. The CINEMA MULTIPLEX flagship film applies the same approach to a single scene from a number of disparate film sources. Enjoy the Entertainment!
